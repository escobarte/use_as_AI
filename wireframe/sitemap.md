Project Description:
A comprehensive landing page for a modern logistics and freight company that serves two audiences: shippers looking for transport and professional drivers looking for employment. The design should be clean, trustworthy, and professional.

Sitemap Structure & Section Details:

Header: Navigation bar with links to About, Services, and Blog. Include two distinct Call-to-Action buttons: "Client Login" and "Get a Freight Quote."

Hero Section: A split-focus hero area. Headline about reliable freight delivery. Two primary buttons: "Apply as a Driver" and "View Shipping Services."

Stats Bar: A concise social proof section showing key metrics: total loads delivered, years of experience, and a high driver satisfaction rating.

Services Grid: A section titled "Our Freight Solutions" featuring cards for Full Truckload (FTL), Partial Load (LTL), Express Delivery, and 24/7 Dispatch Support.

Trust & Compliance Section: A content block focusing on transparency and safety. Mention GPS tracking, DOT certification, no hidden fees, and an on-time delivery guarantee. Use minimalist icons.

Testimonials: A "What Our Partners Say" section featuring quotes from shippers and owner-operators to build industry authority.

FAQ Section: An accordion-style list answering common questions about service areas, equipment types, and the driver application process.

Final CTA: A bold closing section titled "Ready to Move With Us?" with a primary button to "Contact Us Today."

Design Style: > Professional, corporate, and high-contrast. Use plenty of white space to ensure readability and a mobile-first approach.
